Illegal Garbage Dumping Detection & Waste Management System

Sep 2025 – Feb 2026

Dump Surveillance AI is an AI-powered platform created to detect, analyze, and manage illegal garbage dumping incidents through intelligent surveillance. The system combines CCTV-based detection with computer vision, OCR, geospatial analysis, and citizen reporting to create a practical response workflow for waste enforcement and public awareness.

  • Uses YOLO-based object detection to identify dumping activities in surveillance footage.
  • Extracts vehicle number plates with OCR and supports incident evidence tracking.
  • Maps hotspots with Leaflet and turns raw incidents into actionable monitoring insights.
  • Includes a reporting flow for citizens and a chatbot that educates users about proper waste disposal.
