Project Deep Dive

CAMS

A role-based campus activity platform designed to streamline event coordination, student participation, and progress tracking in a structured digital environment.

Why it matters

This system makes campus engagement easier to manage by separating responsibilities across admin, faculty, and student roles. It reduces manual effort and gives each stakeholder a clear interface for action and oversight.

What it includes

  • Admin tools for managing staff, students, and campus activities.
  • Faculty workflows for adding activities and verifying participation.
  • Student-facing features for applications, cancellations, and certificates.
  • Reporting features such as activity points and semester-wise summaries.
